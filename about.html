About

The purpose of this website is to be used as a support material during the poster session for Advanced Algorithms 2. The ability to generate an interactive BA scale free network, along with its co-responding global clustering coefficient is presented on the calculator page, while the poster is viewable here. The co-responding nomenclature and references to support the poster are also available on this website. The contributing authors are listed on the footer of this page.
